Two ways to dance

Square or thin. You can't lose.

Every pie starts with our 48-hour cold-proofed dough. Then it goes one of two beautiful directions.

The thick one

Detroit Square

Baked in a seasoned blue-steel pan so the cheese caramelizes against the wall into a lacy, crunchy frico edge. Airy, focaccia-soft middle. Sauce racing-striped over the top.

Tavern Thin
